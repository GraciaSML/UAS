    <div class="container">
        <h2>Ingredients</h2>
        <ul class="ingredients">
            <li>2 cups cooked rice</li>
            <li>2 eggs</li>
            <li>1 cup carrots, shredded</li>
            <li>1 cup snow peas, chopped</li>
            <li>1 cup frozen corn</li>
            <li>2 tablespoons soy sauce</li>
            <li>1 tablespoon sugar</li>
            <li>1 tablespoon oil</li>
            <li>1 teaspoon garlic powder</li>
            <li>1 teaspoon onion powder</li>
            <li>Salt, to taste</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Directions</h2>
        <ol class="directions">
            <li>In a large non-stick skillet, heat the oil over medium heat.</li>
            <li>Add the garlic powder and onion powder. Stir for 1 minute.</li>
            <li>Add the rice, and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes, or until the rice is heated through.</li>
            <li>Add the eggs, and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked through.</li>
            <li>Stir in the carrots, snow peas, frozen corn, soy sauce, sugar, and salt.</li>
            <li>Reduce the heat to low, and cook for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and coated the rice.</li>
            <li>Remove the skillet from the heat, and serve immediately.</li>
        </ol>
